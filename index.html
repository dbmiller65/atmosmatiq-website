About AtmosMatIQ

AtmosMatIQ gives you instant access to accurate weather information with a clean, intuitive interface.

Whether you're planning your day or checking conditions before heading out, AtmosMatIQ provides the essential weather information you need at a glance.

Features

  • Current weather conditions including temperature, humidity, and wind speed
  • Automatic location detection to show weather for your current location
  • Search for any city worldwide to check weather conditions
  • Pull-to-refresh for the latest weather updates
  • Simple, distraction-free design that focuses on what matters
