Introduction

David Miller ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you use the AtmosMatIQ app and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that information.

Information We Collect

When you use AtmosMatIQ, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Location Information: With your permission, we collect your device's location to provide weather information for your current area. You can disable location services for AtmosMatIQ at any time through your device settings.
  • Search History: We temporarily store the cities you search for to improve your experience, but this information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics to help us improve the app, such as which features are most commonly used.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide accurate weather information for your location or searched cities
  • Improve and optimize the app's performance and user experience
  • Diagnose technical problems and maintain the security of the app

Third-Party Services

AtmosMatIQ uses the OpenWeatherMap API to provide weather data. When you search for a city or use location services, we send that location information to OpenWeatherMap to retrieve weather data. Please review OpenWeatherMap's privacy policy at https://openweathermap.org/privacy-policy for information on how they handle data.

Data Retention

We retain location data only for the duration necessary to provide you with weather information. Search history is stored locally on your device and can be cleared by clearing the app's data through your device settings.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, such as the right to access, correct, or delete your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address provided below.

Children's Privacy

AtmosMatIQ is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected or received personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the app or by other means.
